Get Started See the CLI
pip install fleece-cli

What Fleece does

Everything you need to pick the right card

Stop guessing. Fleece pulls live issuer data and translates it into clear, actionable research — instantly.

💳

Full Card Reports

Fees, welcome offers, earning rates, credits, and benefits — all from live sources, not stale training data.

📊

Wallet Analysis

Coverage maps, category overlaps, and gaps across your existing cards. Know exactly which card to swipe.

✈️

Transfer Partners

Airline and hotel partners, transfer ratios, and timing — so you can plan the redemption before you apply.

💰

ROI Calculator

First-year value estimates based on your actual spend: welcome bonus + earn rate + credits minus annual fee.

📰

Live News

Freshness-filtered results from the past month — benefit cuts, fee increases, new perks, limited-time offers.

🤖

Agent-Ready

Every command outputs clean JSON. Drop it into Claude Code, Codex, or any AI agent with --json.

CLI

Research at the speed of your terminal

Nine commands covering every angle of credit card research. Works standalone or piped into your AI workflow.

$ fleece wallet
Analyzing portfolio: Amex Gold · Chase Sapphire Preferred

Category coverage:
dining 4x MR (Amex Gold)
groceries 4x MR (Amex Gold, up to $25k)
chase travel 5x UR (CSP)
other travel 2x UR (CSP)
everything else 1x ← gap

Suggested next card: Chase Freedom Unlimited (1.5x UR catch-all)

$ fleece roi "Amex Gold" --dining 800 --other 1200
First-year value: $1,340 (bonus $800 + earn $790 + credits $240 − fee $250)
