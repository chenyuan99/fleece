Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026  ·  Fleece for iOS

Fleece does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. All processing happens on your device.

This Privacy Policy describes how Fleece ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use the Fleece iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Fleece does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account required. You do not create an account or provide an email address to use Fleece.
  • No analytics. We do not use any analytics SDK, crash reporting service, or usage tracking.
  • No advertising. We do not serve ads and do not share data with ad networks.
  • No server. Fleece has no backend server. No data is sent to us at any point.

Location Data

Fleece requests access to your device's location to identify nearby merchants and recommend the best credit card for your purchase.

  • On-device only. Your location is processed entirely on your iPhone using Apple's MapKit framework. It is never transmitted to Fleece or any third-party server we control.
  • Apple MapKit. Place lookups use MKLocalSearch, which may send your location to Apple's servers as part of Apple's own services. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • When in use only. Location is accessed only while the app is open ("When In Use"). We do not request background location access.
  • Never stored. Location data is used in the moment to find nearby places and is not saved to disk, iCloud, or any database.

Wallet Data

The cards you add to your Fleece wallet are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any Fleece server
  • May be included in iCloud device backups if you have iCloud Backup enabled — governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Apple Wallet & PassKit

Fleece uses Apple's PassKit framework to detect which payment networks (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) are active in your Apple Wallet. This detection:

  • Returns only a yes/no answer per network — no card numbers, names, or balances are accessible
  • Is performed on-device using iOS system APIs
  • Is not transmitted to Fleece or any third party

Apple Intelligence

On devices running iOS 26 or later with Apple Intelligence enabled, Fleece may use the on-device language model to generate short explanations of card recommendations.

  • All inference runs entirely on-device using Apple's Foundation Models framework
  • No prompts or responses are sent to Fleece or any external server
  • This feature is fully optional — the app works identically without it

Push Notifications

Fleece uses local notifications (not remote push notifications) to alert you when you enter a store. These notifications:

  • Are generated entirely on-device — no notification server is involved
  • Contain only locally derived information (place name, card recommendation)
  • Can be disabled at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → Fleece

Third-Party Services

Fleece does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The only external service involved is Apple's MapKit for place lookups, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Fleece is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because we collect no personal information from any user, this policy applies equally to all age groups.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users through the App Store release notes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
