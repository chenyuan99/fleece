## Rewards Currency Cross-Reference

Useful when the user has a specific points program and wants to know which cards feed into it.

### Chase Ultimate Rewards (UR)
Transferable cards: Sapphire Reserve, Sapphire Preferred, Sapphire Reserve for Business, Ink Business Preferred  
Cash-back UR (transferable only when paired): Freedom Unlimited, Freedom Flex, Ink Business Cash, Ink Business Unlimited  
Cash-back UR (not transferable): Ink Business Premier

Transfer partners (1:1 unless noted): United, Southwest, Air Canada Aeroplan, Air France/KLM Flying Blue, British Airways Avios, Iberia Avios, Aer Lingus, Emirates Skywards, Singapore KrisFlyer, Virgin Atlantic, Hyatt, Marriott (1:1 but poor value), IHG

### American Express Membership Rewards (MR)
All MR cards transfer at same ratios.  
Cards: Platinum, Gold, Green (personal); multiple Amex business cards not in this KB

Transfer partners (1:1 unless noted): Air Canada Aeroplan, Air France/KLM Flying Blue, ANA Mileage Club, British Airways Avios, Cathay Pacific Asia Miles, Delta SkyMiles, Emirates Skywards, Etihad Guest, Hawaiian Miles, Iberia Avios, JetBlue TrueBlue (250:200), Qantas Frequent Flyer, Singapore KrisFlyer, Virgin Atlantic, Choice Hotels (1:1), Hilton Honors (1:2), Marriott (1:1 but poor value)

### Capital One Miles
Cards: Venture X, Venture  
Transfer partners (1:1): Air Canada Aeroplan, Air France/KLM Flying Blue, Avianca LifeMiles, British Airways Avios, Emirates Skywards, Etihad Guest, EVA Air, Finnair, Singapore KrisFlyer, Turkish Miles&Smiles, Virgin Red; Accor (2:1 poor value)

### Citi ThankYou Points
Transferable cards: Strata Premier (and Prestige, not in KB)  
Cash-back only without pairing: Double Cash  
Transfer partners (1:1): Air France/KLM Flying Blue, Avianca LifeMiles, Cathay Pacific Asia Miles, Emirates Skywards, Etihad Guest, EVA Air, Qantas, Singapore KrisFlyer, Turkish Miles&Smiles, Virgin Atlantic, Wyndham (1:1)

### Bilt Rewards
Cards: Bilt Mastercard  
Transfer partners (1:1): Air Canada Aeroplan, Air France/KLM Flying Blue, Alaska Mileage Plan, American AAdvantage, British Airways Avios, Cathay Pacific Asia Miles, Emirates Skywards, Iberia Avios, Turkish Miles&Smiles, United MileagePlus, Virgin Atlantic; Hyatt (1:1), IHG (1:1), Marriott (1:1), Hilton (1:1)  
Note: Rent Day (1st of each month) doubles earn rates for 24 hours.
