benefits guide — amounts and conditions can change at benefit renewal.

## Rental Car Coverage

**Primary coverage:** pays before your personal auto insurance. Avoids a personal insurance claim.  
**Secondary coverage:** only pays what your personal insurance doesn't; still requires filing with personal insurer first.

## Key Strategic Notes

- **CSR vs CSP:** Reserve wins on trip delay trigger (6h vs 12h) and rental car max ($75k); both have identical trip cancellation limits. The $455 incremental fee gap is not justified by protections alone — also factor in $300 travel credit and lounge access.
- **Amex gap:** Amex Gold lacks trip cancellation insurance and provides only secondary rental coverage — significant gaps for a $325 card. The Platinum has better protections but still secondary rental.
- **Bilt punch above weight:** Primary rental CDW, trip cancellation up to $5k, and cell phone protection on a $0 annual fee card makes it an ideal "pay the hotel/car" card for protections.
- **Always charge the full booking to the card** — partial charges may not trigger coverage.
