# Lounge Access by Card

Which cards provide access to which lounge networks, guest policies, and key limitations.

---

## Centurion Lounges (American Express)

Locations: ATL, BOS, CLT, DEN, DFW, HKG, IAH, JFK, LAX, LGA, LHR, MCO, MIA, MSP, PHL, PHX, SEA, SFO, SIN (expanding)

| Card | Access | Guests | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Amex Platinum (personal) | Yes | $50/guest | 2025+: spend $75k+/year on card for unlimited guest access; otherwise pay-per-guest |
| Amex Business Platinum | Yes | $50/guest | Same guest fee policy |
| Amex Centurion (Black) | Yes | 2 complimentary guests | Invite-only card |

**Key limitation (effective 2025):** Amex has tightened guest policies. Cardholders who don't spend $75,000+/year on the Platinum card are charged $50/guest. The Centurion cardholder cap on free guests is 2.

---

## Priority Pass Select

Largest independent lounge network — 1,400+ lounges worldwide across all major airlines and independents.

| Card | Access | Guest Policy | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Amex Platinum | Priority Pass Select | $35/guest after free limit | Enrollment required via Amex; restaurant credit included at some PP locations |
| Chase Sapphire Reserve | Priority Pass Select | $35/guest (no free guests for cardholder's account by default) | Enrollment required; includes some non-lounge restaurants and experiences |
| Capital One Venture X | Priority Pass Select | 2 free guests; $35/each after | One of the more generous guest policies |
| United Club Business card | United Club membership | Guests included (one at a time, or immediate family) | Full United Club membership, not just Priority Pass |

**Note:** Priority Pass has two tiers: "Select" (unlimited visits, guests at $35) and full membership. Most card-provided access is Priority Pass Select.

## Capital One Lounges

Locations: DEN (Denver), DFW (Dallas/Fort Worth), IAD (Washington Dulles), JFK (New York — opening)

| Card | Access | Guest Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Capital One Venture X | Unlimited free access | 2 free guests per visit |
| Capital One Venture (personal) | 2 free visits/year | Guests at $45/visit after free visits |
| Capital One Spark (business) | 2 free visits/year | Same |

Capital One Lounges also give Venture X cardholders access via **Priority Pass** at non-Capital One locations.

|---|---|---|
| Chase Sapphire Reserve | Unlimited free access | Limited complimentary guests; additional guests charged |

| Sapphire Reserve for Business | Unlimited free access | Same policy as personal Reserve |

## United Club

Locations: 45+ United hubs and major airports; plus partner lounges (Star Alliance) internationally

| Card | Access Type | Guest Policy |
|---|---|---|
| United Club Business | Full United Club membership (included in fee) | 1 adult guest OR immediate family (spouse + children under 21) |
| United Business (non-Club) | No Club access | — |

United Club members also access **Star Alliance lounges** when flying internationally on a Star Alliance carrier.

---

## Delta Sky Club

Locations: 50+ Delta hubs and major airports

| Card | Access | Guest Policy | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Amex Platinum | Limited — 10 visits/year (2025 cap) | Guests charged $50/visit | Spend $75k+ on Platinum card for unlimited access |
| Delta Reserve (personal) | Limited visits/year based on card tier | Guests at $50/visit | Reserve gives more visits than lower Delta cards |
| Delta Reserve Business | Same as personal Reserve | Same | |

**Key limitation:** Delta significantly restricted lounge access in 2023–2025. Non-Delta cardholders accessing via Amex Platinum are now capped at 10 complimentary visits/year unless they meet the $75k spend threshold.
