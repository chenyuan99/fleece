# Personal Credit Cards

Each card entry uses consistent fields:
- **annual_fee** — USD
- **earn_rates** — category: multiplier (X = points per dollar, % = cash back)
- **spend_cap** — earn rate limit, if any
- **travel_portal_bonus** — whether elevated rate requires booking through issuer portal
- **rewards_currency** — points/miles type and transferability
- **notable_perks** — credits and benefits that affect effective annual fee

## Chase Sapphire Preferred

- **annual_fee:** $95
- **earn_rates:**
  - 5X Chase Travel (portal)
  - 3X dining, select streaming services, online grocery (excluding Target, Walmart, wholesale clubs)

- **spend_cap:** none
