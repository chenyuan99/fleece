# Business Credit Cards — Chase

Each card entry uses consistent fields:
- **annual_fee** — USD
- **earn_rates** — category: multiplier (X = points per dollar)
- **spend_cap** — earn rate limit, if any
- **chase_travel_bonus** — whether elevated rate applies specifically on Chase Travel portal
- **rewards_currency** — points/miles type and transferability

---

## Sapphire Reserve for Business

- **annual_fee:** $795
- **earn_rates:**
  - 8X Chase Travel (portal)
  - 4X direct flights, direct hotels
  - 3X social media advertising, search engine advertising
  - 1X all other purchases
- **spend_cap:** none
- **chase_travel_bonus:** yes — 8X on Chase Travel portal
- **rewards_currency:** Chase Ultimate Rewards (UR) — transferable to airline/hotel partners
