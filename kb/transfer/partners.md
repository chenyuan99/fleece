# Transfer Partners

Offline reference for all major US transferable points programs.
Fields: partner name · transfer ratio · estimated transfer time · notes

---

## Chase Ultimate Rewards (UR)

Eligible cards: Sapphire Reserve, Sapphire Preferred, Ink Business Preferred  
(Freedom Unlimited, Freedom Flex, Ink Cash, Ink Unlimited require pairing with an eligible card)

### Airline Partners

| Partner | Ratio | Transfer Time | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Air Canada Aeroplan | 1:1 | Instant | Star Alliance; excellent for partner awards (ANA, Lufthansa, United) |
| Aer Lingus AerClub | 1:1 | Instant | Oneworld; Avios currency — pools with BA/Iberia/Vueling |
| Air France/KLM Flying Blue | 1:1 | Instant | SkyTeam; monthly Promo Rewards often 25–50% off awards |
| British Airways Executive Club | 1:1 | Instant | Oneworld Avios; distance-based pricing cuts both ways |
| Emirates Skywards | 1:1 | 1–2 days | Best for Emirates metal; limited partner redemptions |
| Iberia Plus | 1:1 | Instant | Oneworld Avios; cheapest transatlantic on own metal |
| Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer | 1:1 | 1–2 days | Star Alliance; top-tier for Singapore Suites and partner premium cabins |
| Southwest Rapid Rewards | 1:1 | Instant | No partner or alliance awards; best for Companion Pass strategy |
| United MileagePlus | 1:1 | Instant | Star Alliance; close-in saver availability; good for international partners |
| Virgin Atlantic Flying Club | 1:1 | Instant | SkyTeam/partner; best value for ANA and Delta premium cabins |

### Hotel Partners

| Partner | Ratio | Transfer Time | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| World of Hyatt | 1:1 | Instant | Best hotel transfer in UR ecosystem; ~1.7–2.0 cpp at aspirational properties |
| IHG One Rewards | 1:1 | Instant | Moderate value; best at IHG properties with 4th night free benefit |
| Marriott Bonvoy | 1:1 | Instant | Generally poor value; avoid unless targeting a specific Marriott category |
